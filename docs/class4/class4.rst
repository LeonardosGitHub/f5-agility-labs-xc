F5 Distributed Cloud 201 - Intro to Multi-Cloud Networking
==========================================================

This Lab environment highlights some of the basic concepts of F5 Distributed Cloud Multicloud Networking.

During the lab you will be emulating a customer that needs to provide connectivity from and between their physical Data Center and two Public Cloud providers. 
The goal is to demonstrate and understand when to use Network Connect or App Connect to securely extend connectivity between disparate environments. 

.. Caution:: Please be aware that there is a waiting period after Lab 1, while provisioning occurs. If you are an instructor, please proceed with Lab 1 prior to any presentation to give ample time for processes to complete.
